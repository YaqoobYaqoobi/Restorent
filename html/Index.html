Our Journey

In a small, sunlit kitchen in the heart of Italy, two friends,
Marco and Sofia, were experimenting with food. Marco, a
third-generation pasta maker, was passionate about
traditional Italian recipes. Sofia, a world traveler, had fallen
in love with the bold flavors and portability of Mexican
burritos during her adventures.
